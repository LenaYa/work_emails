Hi all!

Throughout a year we’ve been strictly following the project’s roadmap, delivering its key points on time. And product launch is no exception. Today, after months of continuous effort and hard work, we proudly present the beta-version of The Abyss Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

The MVP is the first implementation of The Abyss digital distribution platform with a starting pack of online video games, multilevel referral program, dual billing system with crypto/fiat support, and many other features. Our goal was to apply and develop best practices in terms of technology and user experience, and we've made a great progress doing this. MVP is a very powerful vision for our future and the future of the entire video game industry. It is clearly designed, adaptive, user-friendly and... already launched.

The Abyss adopts an innovative Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) model helping to attract more users to the crypto industry. We offer game developers both flexible and powerful set of tools for developing and publishing casual and/or blockchain games. The Abyss also provides a transparent crypto-friendly payment system and marketing support for user acquisition. Our key feature is a multilevel referral program that serves as an additional revenue stream of ABYSS tokens and a win-win solution for gamers and developers.

The Abyss MVP release note:

  • Starting pack of ready-to-play browser games
  • Adaptive dark-moon design
  • Crypto wallet support
  • Token/fiat payments support
  • Token balance top-up functionality
  • User Account profile with 2FA authentication
  • Operational multilevel referral program
  • Referral link and statistics
  • Token balance and transaction history
  • Game descriptions, screenshots, videos
  • Dynamic call-to-action buttons
About The Abyss

The Abyss is a digital distribution game platform for MMO and multiplayer video games. The Abyss offers multiple income-generating opportunities available through blockchain-powered services and rewards users with ABYSS tokens for social and gaming activities. The Abyss brings cryptocurrency mass adoption to the video game industry in a legal and easy-to-use way.
